Bio

A band originally conformed of 4 band members which was born in England in the late 1960´s has been ever since the most talented exponents of the rock genre. Many critics claim this band is one of the most successful, innovative and influential bands of all times, having sold hundreds of millions of record copies worldwide. Holders of many number-one spots in the UK and the US are unquestionably one of the biggest names in the music world and one that has been immortalized by their incredibly rich sounds and majestic lyrics.

Band Members:

  • Robert Plant - Vocalist

  • Jimmy Page - Guitarist

  • John Paul Jones - Bassist/Keyboardist

  • John Boham - Drummer

Having been in the industry for over 10 years, this was just enough time to create the best albums ever recorded.

The band´s 13 year trajectory was more than enough to produce the best sounds and creative lyrics. Having produced 9 albums, which sold around 200-300 million units throughout the world. The band is certified as the third best-selling band and fifth best-selling act in the Unites States.

Each of their 9 albums have been in the top 10 of the Billboard album chart and six became the leader of the list being the number one spot.
